Business Comparison Tool - Embed Demo

How to Embed the Business Comparison Tool

You can easily embed the Business Comparison Tool on your website by adding a container element with the ID "business-tool" and including our embed script. The tool will automatically render inside the container.

New! Our embeddable tool now includes AI analysis that provides actionable insights for your business based on your Google Maps data, competitor analysis, and SEO metrics.

Google Maps Integration

Simply enter a Google Maps business URL and get a complete analysis of your business and competitors.

SEO Analysis

Get insights into your website's SEO performance compared to competitors.

AI-Powered Insights New

Receive intelligent recommendations and a complete AI analysis of your business strengths, weaknesses, and growth opportunities.

Basic Embed Code

Add this code to your website where you want the Business Comparison Tool to appear:

<div id="business-tool"></div>
<script src="https://your-domain.com/embed.js"></script>

How to Use the Embedded Tool

  1. Find your Google Maps Business URL - Search for your business on Google Maps and copy the URL from your browser
  2. Paste the URL - Enter the URL in the form field
  3. Analyze - Click the "Analyze Business" button to get your full business analysis
  4. Review Results - See your business metrics, competitor analysis, and AI-powered recommendations
  5. AI Insights - Check the AI Insights section for personalized growth strategies and competitive analysis

  • Business Strengths - Identify what's working well for your business

Customization Options

You can customize the appearance of the embedded tool by adding data attributes to the container element:

  • data-primary-color: Set the primary accent color (e.g., "#3b82f6")
  • data-secondary-color: Set the secondary accent color (e.g., "#10b981")
  • data-height: Set a custom height for the iframe (e.g., "800px")
  • data-theme: Set the theme to "light" or "dark" (default is "dark")

Example with Customization

<div 
  id="business-tool"
  data-primary-color="#3b82f6"
  data-secondary-color="#10b981"
  data-height="800px"
  data-theme="dark"
></div>
<script src="https://your-domain.com/embed.js"></script>
